Create your own flashcards easily

Xcard integrates with your Google account, you can import a Google spreadsheet to Xcard directly. You don't need to make flashcards one by one anymore, can create your own flashcards easily.

Concentrate on your learning

The way to study is very simple. If you remember the meaning of a card, tap OK button, if you don't, tap NG button. You can check the meaning by tapping the card.

Check your records of studying

You can check the summary of studying, such as the count of study, transition of correct rate, and so on. Please use this to keep your motivation to study.
